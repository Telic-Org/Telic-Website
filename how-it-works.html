From process to production

Structure the work. Automate the routine. Handle the exceptions.

Telic turns an existing business process into a controlled workflow that can execute repeatedly, use intelligence selectively, and stop safely when human input is needed.

The operating cycle

How a Telic workflow moves from input to verified result.

01

Map the process

Define steps, decisions, expected results, exceptions, and review points.

02

Connect the environment

Prepare the applications, files, and working sessions involved.

03

Execute the work

Run known steps using reliable, previously verified paths.

04

Handle variation

Apply AI or human guidance only to the uncertain part.

05

Verify and record

Check the result and preserve a complete operational history.

Selective intelligence

AI is not asked to rediscover the workflow on every run.

Routine actions follow the known process. AI is introduced when changing information, ambiguity, or a recoverable exception creates a genuine need for interpretation.

01

Document understanding

Interpret invoices, forms, orders, correspondence, and other changing inputs.

02

Ambiguity resolution

Choose between plausible records or paths using the relevant workflow context.

03

Exception recovery

Respond to unexpected screens, changed controls, and familiar operational interruptions.

04

Human escalation

Pause and present the exact decision, evidence, and action needed from a person.

Safe continuation

Every meaningful step ends with evidence, not assumption.

Telic verifies that the expected outcome happened. If the result is unclear—especially after a consequential action—it can inspect further, request human confirmation, or stop rather than repeating the action blindly.

EXPECTED RESULT

Know what success means

Each step has a clear intended outcome rather than relying on an agent deciding that it looks finished.

CURRENT EVIDENCE

Check the actual state

Telic uses the available result, screen, record, or application state to determine what happened.

SAFE ROUTE

Continue, recover, or ask

The workflow only moves on when the next action is appropriate for the confirmed state.
